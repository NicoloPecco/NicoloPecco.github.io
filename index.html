About

I am a PhD researcher in Neuroscience and Experimental Neurology with a strong background in biomedical engineering. My research focuses on artificial intelligence methods for medical imaging, with particular emphasis on brain MRI, glioma segmentation and characterization, and fetal neurodevelopment.

I work on deep learning, radiomics, multimodal MRI, inter-rater variability analysis, and explainable AI for clinical decision support.

Research Interests

  • Deep learning for medical image analysis
  • Glioma segmentation and molecular characterization
  • Fetal and neonatal brain MRI
  • Radiomics and diffusion/perfusion MRI
  • Model interpretability and uncertainty
